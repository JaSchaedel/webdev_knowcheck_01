About Me

I am a creative soul who is passionate about expressing myself through both art and technology. My journey began with a love for painting and writing, where I've found joy in bringing my imagination to life on canvas and paper. However, as the digital world evolves, I've found myself drawn to the endless possibilities that technology can provide. Learning to code is a new and exhilarating challenge for me. It's a new form of art, mixing my love of writing with the power of colors and shapes. Instead of pencils, brushes, and paints, I use lines of code to craft digital masterpieces. With each line of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I'm discovering new ways to bring my creative visions to life.
